# Inventory Snapshots

Inventory Snapshots allow you to view the state of inventory **as it was on a specific past date**. The system retains a history of up to **three months**.

Unlike the real-time inventory report, this report preserves a record of how stock levels looked at different points in time.

## Using Snapshots

- Select a past date to view the inventory as it stood at that moment.
- Apply filters to narrow down the results by product or other criteria.
- **Export** the filtered results to Excel.
